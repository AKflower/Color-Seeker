Color Seeker

How to Play "Color Seeker"

Your goal is to find the tile with a slightly different color from the others before time runs out. A grid of tiles will appear, and you need to click on the one that looks different. If you find it, you’ll move to the next level with a larger grid. Keep an eye on the green time bar—if it runs out, it’s game over! Try to progress as far as you can by quickly spotting the difference. Good luck!
